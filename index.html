UW Drupal Users Group

Who are we?

We are a community of site administrators, developers, designers, and content editors who use the free and open-source content management system Drupal to build web sites and applications at the University of Washington. See this list of sites running on Drupal at UW.

How can you get involved?

Join our mailing list to ask questions and share knowledge with other Drupal users at UW. Join our organization on Drupal.org to show your participation. And come to our meetings!

When do we meet?

We normally meet monthly after web council. Check our calendar for upcoming meetings and other Drupal-related events.

Where can you find more resources?

Please visit our wiki for notes from past meetings, community-maintained themes and modules, tips on installing Drupal, and more.

We are in the process of building a new site to make these resources more accessible and visible to the UW community (and beyond). If you'd like to help with that, please email Dan Druliner (druliner@uw.edu) and Heather Wozniak (hwozniak@uw.edu).
